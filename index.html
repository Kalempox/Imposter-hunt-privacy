1. Scope & controller

This policy applies to the Imposter Hunt mobile application (Android and iOS) published under the package names com.taaf.imposterhunt (Android) and com.taaf.imposterhunt (iOS). The data controller for the app is the developer/operator of Imposter Hunt. We do not operate separate servers that store your game data; the app runs locally on your device.

2. Data we collect

We do not collect personal data from you directly. We do not require an account, email, or any sign-in. The app does not send your name, game choices, or player lists to our servers (we have no game servers).

Any information that might be processed by third parties (e.g. advertising or crash reporting) is described in the "Third-party services" section below.

3. Data stored locally on your device

The app stores the following data only on your device (e.g. via the device's app storage or in-memory during a session):

  • Player names you enter for a game
  • Game settings (number of players, number of imposters, round time, difficulty, category)
  • Votes and game state during and after a round
  • Any other in-game data needed to play (e.g. roles, timers)

This data is not transmitted to our servers and is not used by us for analytics or advertising. You can clear it by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

4. Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services, which may collect or process data according to their own policies.

4.1 Google AdMob (advertising)

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements (e.g. interstitial ads). Google may collect and use data to deliver and measure ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (e.g. Android Advertising ID, IDFA on iOS)
  • Approximate location (e.g. country or region)
  • App and device information (e.g. app version, device type, OS)
  • Interaction with ads (e.g. views, clicks)

For details and choices (e.g. opt out of personalized ads, reset Advertising ID), see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps
  • On your device: Settings → Google → Ads (or equivalent) to manage ad personalization and reset your Advertising ID.

4.2 Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting)

We use Firebase Crashlytics to receive crash reports and improve app stability. Crashlytics may collect:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device and OS information (e.g. model, OS version)
  • App version and build

This data is processed by Google. For more information:

  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Google Privacy Policy

5. How we use information

We do not use your personal data for our own marketing or profiling. We use:

  • Locally stored game data only to run the game on your device.
  • Third-party data only as governed by those services (e.g. Google for ads and crash reporting). We do not receive or store your name or game content from AdMob or Crashlytics.

6. Data sharing & disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your in-game data (player names, votes, settings) with anyone, because we do not collect or transmit it.

Data that is shared is limited to what is sent to third-party services (AdMob, Crashlytics) as described in section 4. Those providers have their own privacy policies and may use data for their purposes (e.g. ad delivery, analytics, fraud prevention). We may also disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights or safety.

7. Data retention

  • On your device: Local game data remains until you clear app data or uninstall the app.
  • Third parties: Retention of data collected by Google (AdMob, Crashlytics) is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and related product policies.

8. Security

We do not transmit or store your game data on our own servers. We rely on your device's security and on the security practices of the third-party services we use (Google). You are responsible for keeping your device and app updated.

9. Children's privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

10. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Ask what data we hold. We do not hold personal data on our servers; local data is on your device.
  • Deletion: Clear local data by clearing app data or uninstalling. For data held by Google (AdMob, Crashlytics), see Google's privacy and account tools.
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Use your device settings (e.g. "Opt out of Ads Personalization" / "Limit Ad Tracking") and Google's ad settings.
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you are in the EEA/UK or another jurisdiction that provides this right.

11. International users

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, we process data only as described here; we do not collect personal data for our own processing. Your use of the app involves third-party services (Google) that may transfer data internationally; their policies describe their legal bases and safeguards.

If you are in California (USA), we do not sell your personal information. We do not collect personal information beyond what is described in this policy and in our use of AdMob and Crashlytics.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when we do. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
